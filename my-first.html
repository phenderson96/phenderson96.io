How to start a 4430 in the cold.

  1. remove driver's side screen behind fuel tank.
  2. unscrew nut holding cover on air filter.
  3. spray ether into air filter for 2 seconds.
  4. replace cover and screen.
  5. push throttle to halfway open.
  6. press clutch, turn key
